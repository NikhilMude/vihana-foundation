# Vihana Foundation Website Guide

This guide explains how visitors, donors, volunteers and the Vihana Foundation team should use the public website.

## Website URLs

- Temporary Vercel site: `https://vihana-foundation-cgy6.vercel.app`
- Final domain: `https://www.vihanafoundation.org`
- Donor login: `/donor`
- CMS login: `/admin`
- Admin operations dashboard: available automatically after admin login

## Public Website Sections

### Home

The homepage introduces Vihana Foundation with the tagline:

```txt
Small Steps. Lifelong Impact.
```

The homepage can include:

- Hero message and image
- Mission summary
- Programs
- Impact statistics
- Featured story
- Donation section
- Gallery
- Events
- FAQ
- Newsletter signup
- Contact and volunteer forms

Most homepage content is controlled from the CMS.

### About Vihana

Use this page for:

- The story behind Vihana Foundation
- Why the foundation was started
- Values and promise
- Team section
- Founder or trustee details when ready

The Team section is CMS-controlled. You can add, edit, remove and reorder team members from the CMS.

### Mission

Use this page to explain:

- What Vihana Foundation works on
- Who the foundation supports
- The foundation's focus areas
- The long-term vision

### Programs

Use this page to describe program areas such as:

- Education support
- Nutrition and meal drives
- Health and wellness
- Birthday kindness campaigns
- Community outreach

### Impact

Use this page for:

- Impact statistics
- Success stories
- Campaign outcomes
- Donor and volunteer impact

Keep numbers honest and update them only when the foundation has verified records.

### Gallery

Use this page for real photos from:

- Meal drives
- School kit support
- Volunteer activities
- Health camps
- Birthday campaigns
- Community moments

Only upload photos where the foundation has permission to publish them.

### Contact Us

Visitors can submit:

- General enquiries
- Volunteer interest
- Donation questions
- Partnership requests
- Birthday campaign messages

Messages are saved in the CMS and can trigger email notifications when email settings are configured.

### Donate

Visitors can submit donation details. The current system records donations and generates provisional receipts for internal tracking and donor records.

Important:

- Foreign Citizen / OCI donations are currently blocked.
- Official 80G/12A wording should be updated only after legal verification.
- Payment details should be replaced with verified bank/UPI details before public launch.

### Legal Pages

The site includes:

- Privacy Policy
- Terms & Conditions
- Cookie Policy
- Disclaimer
- Refund & Cancellation Policy

Review these with a qualified professional before accepting public donations.

## Donor Account

Donors can:

- Create an account
- Login from the Donor Login button
- See their profile
- Update phone, PAN and address
- Change password
- View donation history
- Download receipt PDFs

If the same donor later gives offline or through admin entry, matching is attempted using email and name.

## Cookies

The cookie banner lets visitors choose:

- Accept all
- Essential only
- Reject all

Cookie consent is stored in the browser.

## Best Practice Before Launch

- Replace dummy UPI, bank details and QR code with verified details.
- Add official registration, PAN, 80G and 12A information only after verification.
- Replace generated sample photos with real consent-approved photos.
- Test contact form, newsletter, donation form and receipt download.
- Test the website on mobile.
- Confirm email delivery from the verified domain.
- Connect domain and SSL.
- Keep the launch banner active until the final site is approved.
